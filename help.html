Help Center

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Sign In" button in the bottom right corner of the chat interface. From there, you can create a new account by providing a username, email, and password.
How do I join a chat?
Once you're signed in, you can join any chat by clicking on the chat name in the list. If you know the specific chat ID, you can enter it directly in the address bar.

Chat Features

How do I send a private message?
To send a private message, click on a user's name in the visitors list, then select "Private Message" from the menu. You can also use the /p command followed by the username and your message.
How do I use smileys?
Click on the smiley icon above the text input area to open the smiley selector. You can also type the smiley code directly, such as (smile) or (cool).
How do I format my text?
You can use the formatting tools by selecting text and clicking on the formatting options. You can also use markdown-style formatting:
  • *text* for bold
  • _text_ for italic
  • ~text~ for strikethrough

Account Management

How do I change my password?
Go to Settings > Account > Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password and then your new password.
How do I change my display name?
Go to Settings > Profile > Display Name. Enter your new display name and save the changes.

Troubleshooting

I can't connect to the chat
Make sure your internet connection is working properly. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, try using a different browser.
I'm experiencing lag or slow performance
This could be due to a slow internet connection or high server load. Try closing other applications that might be using your internet connection. If you're in a very active chat with many users, consider joining a less crowded chat.
